ABOUT THE ARTIST

Hi, I am Paul Alexander.

An ambitious art student and graphic designer aspiring to create something great. Currently, I am based in the Kitchener-Waterloo Area, studying Global Business & Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo.

I grew up watching old cartoons and playing video games, which have been a huge inspiration for my work today. My goal as an artist and a creator is to give that same feeling I felt everyday immersed in those digital worlds to those who enjoy my work.
